I attended school in Aurora, Colorado called Vista Peak Preparatory from 2010 to 2014. The school introduced into the district a varied class structure. Beyond the general education classes an option for 'specialization' or 'pathway learning' was introduced. The options were:

  • Art
  • Business
  • Medicine
  • S.T.E.M.
I chose the S.T.E.M path and learned a lot while I was there. I was introduced to 3D printing, AutoCAD and even hardware assembly through soldering.
